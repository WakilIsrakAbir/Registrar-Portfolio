Hi, I’m Major General Md
Anwarul Islam
SUP, ndu, psc (Retd.)

Major General Md Anwarul Islam (Retd.) is a distinguished former senior officer of the Bangladesh Army with 32 years of honorable service from 1987 to 2020. Throughout his military career, he held key command and strategic appointments at formation and Army Headquarters levels, contributing to operational planning, defense administration, and leadership development. Currently serving as Registrar of Southeast University, he continues to shape future generations through academic governance and institutional leadership.
